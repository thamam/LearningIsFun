## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Voice Not Working?
- Check microphone permissions in browser settings
- Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
- Ensure microphone is connected and working
- Reload the page and allow permissions again

### Speech Recognition Issues?
- Speak clearly at normal speed
- Reduce background noise
- Use headphones with built-in microphone
- Try rephrasing - the app is flexible!

### Progress Not Saving?
- Enable cookies and localStorage in browser
- Don't use private/incognito mode
- Check browser isn't blocking storage
